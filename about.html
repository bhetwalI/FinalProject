About Me

I am Isha Bhetwal, a bright and compassionate individual hailing from the scenic town of Surunga, Jhapa, Nepal.

I am currently pursuing my studies at The British College in the vibrant city of Kathmandu, my inquisitive nature and open-mindedness makes me standout among my peers. I find solace in exploring new places and I cherish the joy of sightseeing, immersing myself in the beauty of the world. When not discovering new horizons, I embrace the healing power of music, my playlist echoing a symphony of emotions. Moreover, I seek knowledge and enrichment through the pages of myfavorite books, allowing the stories to transport me to realms beyond imagination.

Above all, my genuine belief in the power of kindness shines through, inspiring those around me to embrace compassion and understanding in their own journeys.
